Come join us!

Interested? Like singing? Want to be a part of this? Well you too can join!

Our group is open to everyone—that means both UW Undergraduate and Graduate students, Alumni, and even Non-Students! The only requirements are enthusiasm and a good sense of fun. We do require that you attend a certain percentage of rehearsals, so if you join late in the season, you might not be able to sing in the next concert.

There are no auditions for Unleashed, we invite you to show up at any of our rehearsals. You might want to email us though for more information beforehand.

Small Group Squad!

Depending on the size of the main group, some quarters there is also an audition-only small group that meets twice as often and learns a bunch of additional songs. Usually auditions are held at the beginning of the season, but occasional spots might open up at other times.
