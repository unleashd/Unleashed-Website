Hello! Welcome to the homepage of Unleashed!

We are the premier a cappella group at the University of Washington, located just north of beautiful downtown Seattle.

Our group was founded in 1999, for all who love singing a cappella and having fun! Everyone who is interested may join, including graduate students, alumni, and non-students.

Traditionally, we have two concerts every year, a Winter and a Spring concert. In addition, we have other fun events throughout the year, including:

  • Caroling in Downtown Seattle (Figgy Pudding)
  • Performances within the community, such as at retirement homes or hospitals.
  • Participation at school events like Dawg Daze.
  • Retreats and parties.
